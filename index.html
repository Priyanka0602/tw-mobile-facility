INDIA OUTBOUND LONG TERM ASSIGNMENT TRAVEL

We provide the following 3 options to employees on long term assignments
Option 1 De-activate the current number and get a new number when you come back.
Option 2 Change the connection type from corporate to personal and use it for any member of your family.
Option 3 Keep the number activated with basic rental plan Rs.149 + taxes per month on TW account and you can pay actual bill amounts to TW once your back.
